Our story

Your Neighbors, Your Pest Control Experts

To provide Dallas homeowners with safe, effective, and affordable pest control solutions delivered with honesty and respect.

Total Pest Control South Dallas was founded in 2010 by a team of local pest management professionals who saw a need for reliable, customer-focused service in the southern Dallas area. Starting with just one truck and a commitment to doing the job right, we grew through word-of-mouth and a reputation for showing up on time, communicating clearly, and solving problems permanently.

Today, we serve hundreds of homes across Dallas County. We're proud to be a family-owned business that treats every customer like a neighbor. Our technicians live and work in the communities they serve, so they understand the unique pest pressures of North Texas. We invest in ongoing training, eco-friendly products, and the latest equipment to deliver results you can trust.
