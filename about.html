Our story

Dedicated to Keeping Dallas Homes Pest-Free

To provide safe, effective, and affordable pest control solutions that protect our neighbors and their homes.

Total Pest Control South Dallas was founded with a simple mission: deliver reliable, honest pest control to the community we call home. Our founder, a Dallas native, saw a need for a company that treats customers like family and pests like the enemy. Starting with a single truck and a commitment to excellence, we've grown into a trusted name in South Dallas.

Today, we serve hundreds of homes across Dallas and the surrounding areas. Our team of certified technicians stays up-to-date with the latest pest control innovations to ensure we provide the best possible service. We believe in transparency, integrity, and a job well done.
