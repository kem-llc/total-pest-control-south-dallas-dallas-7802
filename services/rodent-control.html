Rodent Control

Humane and effective removal of rats and mice from your property.

Schedule this service

Overview

Rodents can spread diseases and cause property damage. Our rodent control service focuses on exclusion, trapping, and sanitation to remove rodents and prevent them from returning. We seal entry points and provide ongoing monitoring.

What's included

  • Comprehensive exclusion and sealing
  • Humane trapping and removal
  • Sanitation and cleanup services
  • Prevention strategies to keep rodents out

How it works

1

Inspection

We identify rodent entry points, nesting sites, and food sources both inside and outside your home.

2

Exclusion & Trapping

We seal gaps and holes, then set traps in strategic locations to capture rodents.

3

Cleanup & Prevention

We remove droppings and contaminated insulation, then recommend ongoing prevention measures.

Common questions

Do you use poison?

We prefer non-poisonous methods like traps and exclusion to avoid risks to pets and children. In severe cases, we may use baits in secure stations.

How long does rodent control take?

Initial exclusion and trapping can be completed in a day, but full elimination may take 1-2 weeks.
