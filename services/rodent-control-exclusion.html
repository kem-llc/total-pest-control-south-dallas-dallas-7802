Rodent Control & Exclusion

Remove mice and rats and prevent them from coming back with our exclusion services.

Schedule this service

Overview

Rodents can cause structural damage and spread disease. Our rodent control program includes trapping, baiting, and full exclusion – sealing every hole and gap larger than a dime. We also clean and sanitize affected areas.

What's included

  • Comprehensive trapping and removal
  • Full home exclusion – seal entry points
  • Attic and crawlspace inspection included
  • Sanitation and odor removal services

How it works

1

Inspection & Assessment

We locate rodent activity, droppings, gnaw marks, and potential entry points inside and outside your home.

2

Trapping & Baiting

We place tamper-resistant bait stations and traps in safe, strategic locations to eliminate the population.

3

Exclusion & Prevention

We seal all entry points with steel wool, caulk, and heavy-duty materials, then provide ongoing monitoring.
