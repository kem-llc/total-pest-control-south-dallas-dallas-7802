Termite Control & Prevention

Protect your home from termite damage with advanced detection and treatment.

Schedule this service

Overview

Termites cause billions in damage each year. Our termite services include thorough inspections, liquid soil treatments, and baiting systems. We identify early signs of infestation and create a customized protection plan for your property.

What's included

  • Advanced termite detection technology
  • Liquid barrier and bait station options
  • Annual inspections for ongoing protection
  • Damage repair recommendations if needed

How it works

1

Termite Inspection

We examine your foundation, crawlspace, attic, and wood structures for mud tubes, frass, and damage.

2

Treatment Plan

Based on the inspection, we recommend either a liquid termiticide barrier or a Sentricon bait system.

3

Installation & Monitoring

We apply the treatment or install bait stations, then schedule regular monitoring to ensure effectiveness.

Is termite treatment safe for my garden?

Yes, our treatments are targeted and minimize impact on soil and plants. We take care to protect your landscaping.
