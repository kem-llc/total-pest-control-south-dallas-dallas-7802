Bed Bug Heat Treatment

Kill bed bugs at all life stages with our non-chemical heat treatment.

Schedule this service

Overview

Bed bugs are resilient, but heat doesn't lie. Our heat treatment raises the temperature in your home to over 135°F, killing bed bugs and their eggs in one day. No chemicals, no fumes, no hassle. We also offer canine inspections for detection.

What's included

  • 100% chemical-free – safe for families and pets
  • Kills bed bugs and eggs in a single treatment
  • No need to throw away furniture
  • Canine inspection available for accurate detection

How it works

1

Preparation & Inspection

We guide you on preparing your home, then conduct a thorough inspection using K-9 detection if needed.

2

Heat Treatment

Industrial heaters raise the temperature to lethal levels. We monitor sensors throughout the process to ensure even heating.

3

Post-Treatment Verification

After cooling, we inspect again to confirm elimination and provide tips to prevent re-infestation.

How long does heat treatment take?

The entire process typically takes 6–8 hours, plus a few hours for setup and breakdown.

Do I need to leave during treatment?

Yes, you and your pets must vacate during the heat treatment. We'll coordinate a convenient time.
