Overview

Our general pest control service targets a wide variety of common pests that invade Dallas homes. We perform a detailed inspection of your property, identify problem areas, and apply targeted treatments to eliminate pests and prevent their return.

What's included

  • Eliminates ants, roaches, spiders, silverfish, and more
  • Interior and exterior treatment
  • Safe for children and pets
  • Ongoing monitoring and prevention

How it works

1

Common questions

How long does treatment take?

Most general pest treatments take about 30-60 minutes, depending on the size of your home and severity of infestation.

Is the treatment safe for my family?

Yes, we use EPA-approved products that are safe when applied correctly. We recommend staying out of treated areas for a few hours.
