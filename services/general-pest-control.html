General Pest Control

Eliminate ants, roaches, spiders, and more with our comprehensive treatment plans.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our general pest control service covers the most common invaders in Dallas homes. We use a combination of baits, sprays, and exclusion techniques to create a protective barrier around your property. Regular maintenance plans keep pests away year-round.

What's included

  • Targets ants, roaches, silverfish, earwigs, and spiders
  • Interior and exterior treatment included
  • Child- and pet-safe products used
  • Flexible scheduling – monthly, quarterly, or one-time

How it works

1

Inspection

We thoroughly inspect your home to identify pest entry points, nesting areas, and the extent of the infestation.

2

Treatment

Our technician applies targeted treatments to baseboards, cracks, and exterior perimeter. We focus on high-traffic areas.

3

Prevention

We seal gaps, provide recommendations, and schedule follow-ups to ensure pests don't return.
