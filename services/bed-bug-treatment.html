Bed Bug Treatment

Discreet and effective bed bug elimination for homes and businesses.

Schedule this service

Overview

Bed bugs are notoriously difficult to eliminate. Our bed bug treatment combines heat treatments, chemical applications, and thorough inspections to eradicate bed bugs at all life stages. We also provide preparation guidance and follow-up visits.

What's included

  • Heat treatments that kill bed bugs and eggs
  • Chemical spot treatments for targeted areas
  • Minimal preparation required from you
  • Follow-up inspection to ensure success

How it works

1

Inspection

We inspect mattresses, furniture, and crevices to confirm bed bug presence and severity.

2

Treatment

We use a combination of heat (up to 120°F) and EPA-approved insecticides to eliminate bed bugs.

3

Re-Inspection

After 14 days, we return to check for any remaining bed bugs and re-treat if necessary.
